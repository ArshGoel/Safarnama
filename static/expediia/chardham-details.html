Chardham Yatra - 10 Days Itinerary

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Haridwar

  • Reach Haridwar; attend Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.
  • Overnight stay in Haridwar.

Day 2: Haridwar to Barkot

  • Drive to Barkot via Mussoorie and visit Kempty Falls en route.
  • Overnight stay in Barkot.

Day 3: Barkot to Yamunotri

  • Drive to Janki Chatti, and trek 6 km to Yamunotri.
  • Offer prayers at Yamunotri Temple.
  • Return to Barkot for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Barkot to Uttarkashi

  • Drive to Uttarkashi and visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple.
  • Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

Day 5: Uttarkashi to Gangotri

  • Drive to Gangotri and offer prayers at Gangotri Temple.
  • Return to Uttarkashi for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi

  • Drive to Guptkashi via Tehri Dam and check in at the hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

Day 7: Guptkashi to Kedarnath

  • Drive to Gaurikund, and trek (or take a helicopter) to Kedarnath Temple.
  • Attend evening aarti and stay overnight near Kedarnath.

Day 8: Kedarnath to Guptkashi

  • Return to Gaurikund and drive back to Guptkashi.
  • Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

Day 9: Guptkashi to Rishikesh

  • Drive to Rishikesh and explore the local sights.
  • Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 10: Rishikesh to Haridwar

  • Return to Haridwar and end the Yatra with some leisure time.
  • Departure from Haridwar.
