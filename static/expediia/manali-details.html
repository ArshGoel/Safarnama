Manali Trip - 7 Days Itinerary

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Manali

Check-in at the hotel, explore local markets, and stroll through Mall Road.

Day 2: Solang Valley

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding and skiing.

Day 3: Rohtang Pass

Full-day excursion to Rohtang Pass, enjoy the scenic views and snow activities.

Day 4: Cultural Exploration

Explore cultural sites including Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, and Vashisht hot springs.

Day 5: Trekking

Trek to Jogini Waterfalls and visit Old Manali for a quaint experience.

Day 6: Adventure and History

Enjoy river rafting at Kullu and explore the historical Naggar Castle.

Day 7: Leisure and Departure

Free time for shopping and leisure activities, followed by departure.
