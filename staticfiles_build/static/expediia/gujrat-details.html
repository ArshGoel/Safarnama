Gujarat Trip - 7 Days Itinerary

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Ahmedabad

  • Arrive in Ahmedabad and check into your hotel.
  • Visit the Sabarmati Ashram, where Mahatma Gandhi lived.
  • Explore Sidi Saiyyed Mosque, known for its intricate stone latticework.
  • Visit Kankaria Lake for an evening stroll.
  • Overnight stay in Ahmedabad.

Day 2: Ahmedabad to Dwarka

  • Early morning drive to Dwarka (approx. 8 hours).
  • Check-in at the hotel and visit the famous Dwarkadhish Temple.
  • In the evening, attend the Aarti at the temple.
  • Visit Gomti Ghat nearby.
  • Overnight stay in Dwarka.

Day 3: Dwarka Sightseeing

  • Visit Bet Dwarka by boat (considered the home of Lord Krishna).
  • Visit the Rukmini Temple and Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, one of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines.
  • In the evening, enjoy the coastal views and visit Sunset Point.
  • Overnight stay in Dwarka.

Day 4: Dwarka to Somnath

  • Drive to Somnath (approx. 4 hours).
  • En route, visit Porbandar, the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi. Visit Kirti Mandir.
  • Reach Somnath and visit the Somnath Temple, another Jyotirlinga.
  • Attend the evening Aarti and the Sound and Light Show at the temple.
  • Overnight stay in Somnath.

Day 5: Somnath to Gir National Park

  • Early morning visit to Bhalka Tirth, where Lord Krishna is believed to have departed for his heavenly abode.
  • Drive to Gir National Park (approx. 2 hours).
  • Check into a wildlife resort and take an afternoon Jeep Safari to spot the Asiatic lions.
  • Overnight stay near Gir National Park.

Day 6: Gir to Rajkot

  • Enjoy a morning wildlife safari in Gir National Park.
  • After breakfast, drive to Rajkot (approx. 4 hours).
  • Visit the Watson Museum and Kaba Gandhi No Delo, where Mahatma Gandhi spent his early years.
  • Explore Race Course and enjoy a leisurely evening.
  • Overnight stay in Rajkot.

Day 7: Rajkot to Ahmedabad (Departure)

  • Drive back to Ahmedabad (approx. 4 hours).
  • Visit Adalaj Stepwell and Akshardham Temple in Gandhinagar if time permits.
  • Transfer to the airport or railway station for your departure.
