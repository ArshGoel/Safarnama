Goa Trip - 4 Days Itinerary

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Goa

  • Arrive at Dabolim Airport.
  • Transfer to your hotel and check-in.
  • Spend the evening at Candolim Beach or Calangute Beach.
  • Enjoy Goaâ€™s famous seafood at a beach shack.

Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing

  • Visit Fort Aguada and the Chapora Fort.
  • Explore the famous Anjuna Beach and Vagator Beach.
  • In the evening, explore Baga Beach for water sports and nightlife.

Day 3: South Goa Sightseeing

  • Visit Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral in Old Goa.
  • Explore Colva Beach, Benaulim Beach, and the famous Palolem Beach.
  • Take a relaxing boat cruise on the Mandovi River in the evening.

Day 4: Departure

  • Free time for shopping at Panjim Market or a local flea market.
  • Transfer to Dabolim Airport for your departure.
