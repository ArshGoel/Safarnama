Srinagar Trip - 4 Days Itinerary

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Shikara ride on Dal Lake and check-in at the hotel.

Day 2: Mughal Gardens

Visit Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.

Day 3: Day Trip to Gulmarg

Enjoy the Gondola ride and the stunning views.

Day 4: Local Exploration

Explore local markets, enjoy free time, and prepare for departure.
